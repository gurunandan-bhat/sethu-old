We are …

… an award winning non-profit that caters to the developmental, behavioural, emotional and educational needs of children and young people aged 0–19 years through interdisciplinary services.

‘Sethu’ means ‘bridge’ because we build bridges between children and all those who care for them – their families, schools and communities – through collaboration and capacity building.
